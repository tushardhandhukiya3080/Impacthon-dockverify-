The 5-layer model

Five independent
trust anchors.

L1

Cryptographic hash

A keccak256 fingerprint of the raw bytes is stored and compared on every check — one changed pixel and the match fails.

keccak256
L2

Invisible watermark

DWT-DCT steganography embeds an imperceptible signature that survives screenshots and re-compression.

Steganography
L3

Perceptual hash

A visual fingerprint catches look-alikes and edited copies even when the exact bytes differ.

pHash
L4

AI forensics

Google Gemini analyses the document for forgery and screenshot tells, and extracts its key fields automatically.

Gemini Vision
L5

Blockchain record

The proof is written to Ethereum Sepolia — immutable, public, and re-verifiable by anyone, forever.

Ethereum
How it works

Three steps
to certainty.

01

Upload & analyse

Drop in a document. AI extracts its fields and we compute its cryptographic fingerprint in the browser.

02

Anchor on-chain

The file is pinned to IPFS and its hash is written to the DocVerify registry on Ethereum — tamper-evident forever.

03

Verify & unlock

Anyone can re-verify with a QR scan; only the issuer and receiver wallets can unlock the original via MetaMask.
